# Salt Lake City Amplify Scene Concepts

Scene concept ideas for Amplify visualizations at specific locations around Salt Lake City.

---

## 1. The Complex (Downtown SLC)

**Location:** 536 N 400 W, Salt Lake City
A premier multi-room concert venue hosting EDM, hip-hop, and rock acts. Industrial warehouse aesthetic with high ceilings and powerful sound systems.

**Visualization Style:** High-energy laser grids + volumetric fog + strobing geometric projections
**Color Palette:** Electric cyan (#00FFFF), hot magenta (#FF00FF), pure white (#FFFFFF), deep purple (#6600CC)
**SLC Elements:** Incorporate hexagonal patterns echoing the nearby Great Salt Lake's crystalline structure; silhouette outlines of the Wasatch Range skyline
**Use Cases:** Bass music, dubstep, DnB, EDM headliners — artists like Subtronics, Excision, Gramatik would absolutely rock this

---

## 2. Kilby Court (SLC's Backyard Venue)

**Location:** 741 S 500 W, Salt Lake City
Beloved DIY outdoor/indoor venue with an intimate, grass-roots feel. Eclectic booking from indie rock to local hip-hop. Rustic, community-driven atmosphere.

**Visualization Style:** Warm organic particles + hand-drawn style animations + campfire-inspired glow
**Color Palette:** Amber (#FFBF00), warm white (#FFF8E7), burnt orange (#CC5500), sage green (#9DC183)
**SLC Elements:** Utah state flag colors subtly woven in; ambient dust motes simulating Great Salt Lake's famous dust storms on windy days; wildflower motifs referencing nearby hiking trails
**Use Cases:** Indie rock, acoustic, local SLC bands — perfect for acts like The National, Bon Iver, or intimate hip-hop sets

---

## 3. USU Oval / Utah State Capitol Area

**Location:** Central Campus, Logan (or representing the concept in downtown SLC)
Open grassy expanse surrounded by historic buildings. Broad sky visibility, academic energy.

**Visualization Style:** Spherical projection mapping + geometric mandalas + floating geometric prisms
**Color Palette:** Academic maroon (#660000), gold (#FFD700), cream (#FFFDD0), slate gray (#708090)
**SLC Elements:** Pulsing patterns representing the heartbeat of the university; graduation tassel color accents; subtle mountain silhouettes in background
**Use Cases:** University events, graduation ceremonies, large community gatherings, ambient soundscapes for study sessions

## 4. Great Salt Lake Shoreline

**Location:** Antelope Island State Park / Farmington Bay
The largest saline lake in the Western Hemisphere. Vast, desolate, otherworldly. Flamingo Point, Causeway, sunrise/sunset over water.

**Visualization Style:** Reflective particle systems + mirrored surface effects + bioluminescent waves
**Color Palette:** Reflective silver (#C0C0C0), saline white (#F0F8FF), dusty pink (#D4A5A5), horizon gold (#DAA520), deep teal (#008080)
**SLC Elements:** Abstract representations of brine shrimp migration; geometric salt crystal patterns; flight paths of pelicans and American Avocets; the surreal mirror effect when water meets sky
**Use Cases:** Ambient/drone artists, sunrise sets, conceptual electronic — artists like Brian Eno, Tycho, The Blank Pages

---

## 5. Antelope Island

**Location:** Utah's largest island in Great Salt Lake
Wild bison, pronghorn, historic ranch, dramatic mountain backdrop. Rugged, untamed, frontier vibes.

**Visualization Style:** Western horizon silhouettes + dust particle systems + firefly-style bio-luminescence
**Color Palette:** Saddle brown (#8B4513), sunset orange (#FF7F50), buffalo black (#1C1C1C), prairie gold (#D4AF37), desert twilight purple (#4B0082)
**SLC Elements:** Bison silhouette tracking; wild horse running animations; ranch branding iron patterns; star field mapped to actual night sky positions
**Use Cases:** Country, Americana, Western swing — artists like Sturgill Simpson, Orville Peck, traditional folk

---

## 6. Little Cottonwood Canyon

**Location:** Guardsman Pass / Snowbird / Alta
World-class ski terrain, dramatic granite walls, alpine lakes. Iconic rock formations like Devil's Backbone.

**Visualization Style:** Vertical waterfall particles + mountain silhouette wireframes + aurora borealis shimmer
**Color Palette:** Snow white (#FFFAFA), granite gray (#DCDCDC), evergreen (#228B22), powder blue (#B0E0E6), alpenglow pink (#FF6B6B)
**SLC Elements:** Ski trail map projections; climbing route visualizations; aspen leaf cascades; winter storm simulation
**Use Cases:** Après-ski events, EDM on the mountain, ambient electronic — perfect for artists like ODESZA, Big Wild, exMAG

---

## 7. Big Cottonwood Canyon / Brighton

**Location:** Wasatch Back, known for hiking and fall colors
Dense forest, mirror lakes (Silver Lake), vibrant autumn foliage. More accessible, family-friendly recreation area.

**Visualization Style:** Falling leaf particles + reflective water surfaces + gentle wave animations
**Color Palette:** Autumn maple red (#C41E3A), pumpkin orange (#FF7518), golden aspen (#FFC125), forest green (#228B22), crystal blue (#ADD8E6)
**SLC Elements:** Quaking aspen leaf patterns; mirror reflection of forest; trail map overlays; wildlife silhouettes (deer, moose)
**Use Cases:** Folk, jam bands, acoustic Sundays — artists like Trampled by Turtles, Greensky Bluegrass, The Head and the Heart

**Color Palette:** Gaslight yellow (#FFB347), coal black (#1B1B1B), silver mining (#C0C0C0), burgundy (#800020), cream (#FFFDD0)
**SLC Elements:** Silver mining pickaxe and ore cart motifs; historic building outlines; Sundance film reel iconography; ski lift silhouettes
**Use Cases:** Jazz, acoustic soul, singer-songwriters — artists like Norah Jones, Ray LaMontagne, Andrew Bird

**Color Palette:** Urban blue (#4169E1), fountain cyan (#00CED1), concrete gray (#808080), sunset coral (#FF7F7F), neon accent (#39FF14)

## 10. Red Rocks / San Rafael Swell (Southeastern Utah)

**Location:** ~4 hours south of SLC (representing Utah's geological wonders)
Otherworldly canyon lands, towering rock walls, ancient petroglyphs. Stunning natural amphitheaters.

**Visualization Style:** Native american geometric patterns + rock formation projection mapping + desert heat shimmer
**Color Palette:** Desert rust (#B7410E), sandstone (#F4A460), petroglyph red (#8B0000), sky blue (#87CEEB), ancient gold (#CFB53B)
**SLC Elements:** Indigenous pattern motifs (respectful representation); geological stratum layers; shadow play mimicking canyon light at golden hour
**Use Cases:** World music, desert gatherings, transformational festivals — artists like Desert Dwellers, Emancipator, Rising Appalachia

## 11. The Sky (SLC Club)

**Location:** 149 S State Street, Salt Lake City
High-end nightclub with sleek modern interior. Two floors, VIP sections, focus on electronic and hip-hop.

**Visualization Style:** Sleek minimal geometry + pulsing waves + LED strip synchronization
**Color Palette:** Black (#000000) base, chrome (#C0C0C0), electric blue (#7DF9FF), hot pink (#FF69B4), UV reactive white (#F5F5F5)
**SLC Elements:** Subtle ski lift iconography; crystalline patterns; minimal Wasatch line art
**Use Cases:** Top 40 EDM, hip-hop club hits, bottle service vibes — club DJ sets, industry nights

## 12. Utah Lake (Provo Area)

**Location:** Utah Valley's large freshwater lake
The largest freshwater lake in Utah, wetlands, sunset views of the Wasatch Front. Less saline than Great Salt Lake.

**Visualization Style:** Bioluminescent water ripples + lotus flower particle emergence + soft organic waves
**Color Palette:** Lotus pink (#FFB7C5), water teal (#367588), lily pad green (#4A7023), sunset peach (#FFCBA4), mist white (#E6E6FA)
**SLC Elements:** Great White Pelican flight formations; freshwater wetland ecosystems; sunset over Oquirrh and Wasatch ranges; indigenous fish patterns (Bonneville Cutthroat Trout)
**Use Cases:** Ambient electronic, meditation music, yoga sessions, sunrise gatherings — artists like Deep Forest, Peter Kater, Ludovico Einaudi

---

## 13. Temple Square (Downtown SLC)

**Location:** 50 E South Temple, Salt Lake City
Iconic LDS temple, historic Tabernacle, conference center. Sacred, monumental architecture. Global significance.

**Visualization Style:** Respectful projection mapping + light beam columns + subtle geometric reverence
**Color Palette:** Temple white (#FFFFF0), gold (#FFD700), twilight blue (#191970), warm candlelight (#FFB347), granite gray (#DCDCDC)
**SLC Elements:** Temple spire silhouettes; geometric patterns from temple architecture; subtle mountain backdrop integration; historical timeline visualizations
**Use Cases:** Light art installations, community events, cultural presentations — respectful, contemplative, non-religious accessibility
